About Us

We have been friends for many years and share a deep-rooted passion for the hospitality industry. With over 20 years of experience, we have honed our skills across various renowned hotels and restaurants in India, Dubai, and the UK.

In India, we proudly served at several prestigious establishments. Our journey continued in Dubai, where we contributed to the success of a well-known Indian restaurant and bar. This extensive background has equipped us with a wealth of knowledge in decoration, presentation, customer care, and mixology, particularly in crafting exquisite cocktails and mocktails.

Driven by our passion for creating memorable events, we excel in event management services, encompassing everything from decor and fruit and vegetable arrangements to cocktails and welcome drinks. Our dedication to excellence is evident from our local successes within our community, and we are now expanding our services across the UK.

We take pride in bringing joy and satisfaction to our clients through meticulous planning and execution. With us, you can rest assured that your dream events will be handled with the utmost care and creativity. Your happiness is our priority, and we promise an unforgettable experience that exceeds expectations
